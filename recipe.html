How to Make Pizza

(The Next and Final Step)

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough

  • Sauce
  • ~ 1 cup pizza sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • ~ 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ~ 1 tsp oregano
  • ~ Salt and pepper

  • Toppings
  • ~ 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • ~ Pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, olives, peppers, etc.

    • https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/1016230-robertas-pizza-dough

    Step 1: Prepare the Pizza

  • Heat oven to 475°F.
  • Roll dough into a circle or rectangle.
  • Place on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  • Spread sauce evenly.
  • Add cheese

    • Step 2: Bake

  • Bake 12–15 minutes until:
  • crust is golden
  • cheese is melted and bubbly
  • Let cool 2–3 minutes before slicing.

    • * Tips Use a pizza stone for crispier crust. Don’t overload with toppings. Brush crust edges with olive oil for better browning. Easy Dough Formula(This classic pizza dough ratio is:3 cups flour + 1 cup water + 2 1/4 tsp yeast

    Step 3: Add Toppings

  • Popular topping combos:
  • Margherita: mozzarella + basil
  • Meat lovers: sausage + pepperoni + bacon
  • Veggie: mushrooms + peppers + onions + olives
